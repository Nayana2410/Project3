About Us

Our business philosophy is based on offering customers a range of quality products at the best possible prices.

We are service provider & retailer of CCTV, DVR, IP camera, Speed dome, Network surveillance equipment, Proximity Card, GPRS, Fingerprint Reader based Time Attendance system & Web based Access control, Burglar Alarm System, Remote Surveillance system, EAS product, RFID Solutions & other security equipment.

Secam specialises in the design, supply and installation of all types of CCTV systems. We have the expertise and experience to install the appropriate CCTV system for your particular requirements. Single or multi, indoor or outdoor, covert or overt, whatever your surveillance needs. We are equally at home with commercial, industrial, retail or residential assignments, regardless of size. As a matter of principle, the Company only recommends and installs the best quality CCTV equipment available, as well as employing only experienced installers. Secam's products & solutions not only meet technical & functional requirements but also environmental, mechanical & Electrical aspects. Secam provides End-to-End System solutions for CCTV Projects including supplies, installation & commissioning and after sales service support.
