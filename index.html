Secam - A Complete Security Solutions.

We are Super Stockist & Distributor of CCTV, DVR, IP camera, Speed dome, Network surveillance equipment, Video Door Phone and coming with Proximity Card, GPRS, Fingerprint Reader based Time Attendance system & Web based Access control, Burglar Alarm System, Remote Surveillance system, EAS product, RFID Solutions & other security equipment.

Secam specialises in the design, supply and installation of all types of CCTV systems. We have the expertise and experience to install the appropriate CCTV system for your particular requirements. Single or multi, indoor or outdoor, covert or overt, whatever your surveillance needs. We are equally at home with commercial, industrial, retail or residential assignments, regardless of size.
